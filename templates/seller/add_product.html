Tips for a Great Product Listing

Product Name

  • • Be descriptive and specific
  • • Include key features or benefits
  • • Keep it under 60 characters

Description

  • • Highlight key features and benefits
  • • Include dimensions, materials, etc.
  • • Use bullet points for easy reading

Pricing

  • • Research competitor prices
  • • Consider your costs and desired profit
  • • Be competitive but fair

Images

  • • Use high-quality, well-lit photos
  • • Show multiple angles if possible
  • • Avoid watermarks or logos
